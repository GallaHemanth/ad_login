Step-1: New User Registration

1. Valid e-mail address and active mobile number are required.
2. Enter all the relevant details carefully.
3. An OTP will be sent to your registered email address for verification.
4. An OTP will be sent to your Registered Indian Mobile Number for verification.
5. All the correspondence shall be made to the registered email address and mobile number only.

Step-2: Sign in for Registered User

Valid e-mail address and date of birth are required.

Step-3: Filling Online Application Form

1. Fill in the relevant fields carefully.
2. An Temporary Application Number will be generated after filling primary details.
3. Submit this temporary application number to Admission Department at the time of Admission.
